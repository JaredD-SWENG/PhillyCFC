Welcome to Philadelphia Christian Fellowship Church!

We are a small local expression of the Body of Christ in Bucks County, Pennsylvania seeking to glorify God by fulfilling His calling over our lives, our homes and in our church. We meet every Sunday mornings from 10:30am-12pm in our homes as a church. Contact us if you are planning a visit and we will send you the address. All are welcome to join us and to stay for fellowship and lunch immediately after the service.
